## 3. The 3-Trip Process
1. **Initial Inspection:** Meticulous identify of all potential defects using specialized equipment.
2. **Joint Inspection:** Meeting with BSC/MA to walk through and confirm all findings.
3. **Final Rectification:** Verify all defects were properly fixed before you move in.
